About Me

I am Shadrack Musembi, a passionate drummer dedicated to creating powerful rhythms that elevate every musical experience. With a deep love for groove and precision, I specialize in delivering dynamic beats across various styles, from rock and funk to reggae and Afro-inspired rhythms. Whether laying down solid backbeats or exploring intricate fills, my goal is always to bring energy, creativity, and musicality to every performance. Drumming isn't just what I do it's how I connect, express, and inspire.
